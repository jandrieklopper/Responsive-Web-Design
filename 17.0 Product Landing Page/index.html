Unique Destinations

Pentravel wants to get you to places that aren’t along the main tourist trail. We want you to experience different destinations and have unique adventures.

Small Groups

Each tour will have no more than 12 people. This means you get more one-on-one time with the tour guide and so you won’t feel like a number. We want you to connect — with us, the locals and with each other.

Experienced Guides

We organize and host our trips but we’re not the guides. We partner with professionals on the ground in our destinations who have been guiding and operating trips for years. So you’re in excellent hands
